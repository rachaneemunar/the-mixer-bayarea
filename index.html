About

Senior Pro Network

A monthly gathering where senior-care professionals on the Peninsula meet, connect, and grow together.

Our Story

Senior Pro Network was created by a small group of Peninsula professionals (Vivienne Kelvin, Jowett San Miguel, and Mark Martinho) who saw a need: a regular, welcoming space for the people who serve seniors to actually know each other.

What to Expect

Each Senior Pro Network event is hosted at a senior-living community or partner venue on the Peninsula. Expect a two-hour evening of networking and meaningful introductions.

Who Attends

A Community of Trusted Professionals

If you work in senior care, you belong here. Senior Pro Network is an open gathering for the professionals who serve older adults and their families: senior living and memory care, home care and healthcare, move management, financial planning, elder law, and everyone whose work touches the lives of seniors.

There's no membership to join and no invitation to wait for. Come as you are, bring plenty of business cards, and spend an evening among peers who understand the work, passing referrals and resources and building the relationships that help families find the right care at the right time.
