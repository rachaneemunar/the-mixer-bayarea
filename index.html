About

Senior Pro Network

A monthly gathering where senior-care professionals on the Peninsula meet, connect, and grow together.

Our Story

Senior Pro Network was created by a small group of Peninsula professionals — Vivienne Kelvin, Jowett San Miguel, and Mark Martinho — who saw a need: a regular, welcoming space for the people who serve seniors to actually know each other.

Our inaugural Mixer on April 1st, 2026 brought together business leaders, community partners, entrepreneurs, nonprofit professionals, and innovators — all united by a shared passion for supporting our senior community. The energy in the room was electric, and we knew we had something special.

What to Expect

Each Mixer is hosted at a senior-living community or partner venue on the Peninsula. Expect a relaxed two-hour evening of warm conversation, light refreshments, and meaningful introductions.

No formal program. No sales pitches. Just real people who care about seniors, sharing what they do and learning from each other — the old-fashioned, trustworthy way of doing business.

Frequently Asked

Common Questions

What is the purpose of Senior Pro Network?
Senior Pro Network is a small, intentional gathering of senior-service professionals across the Peninsula — senior living, home care, real estate, healthcare, elder law, financial planning, move managers, and the nonprofits that work alongside them. The goal is simple: get to know each other in person, build genuine relationships over time, and eventually pass referrals back and forth with people you actually trust. No pitches, no panels — just real conversations between the people who serve seniors every day.
Is there a cost to attend?
Senior Pro Network is complimentary thanks to our generous hosts and sponsors. We ask only that you register in advance so we can plan for refreshments and seating.
Where and when is the next Mixer?
Our next gathering is Wednesday, July 29th from 4:00–6:00 PM on the rooftop at Harbor at Hillsdale Senior Living — 2883 S. Norfolk St, San Mateo, CA. See the Calendar page for upcoming dates.
Who should attend?
Anyone whose work touches the senior community — senior living operators, home-care providers, SRES realtors, geriatric care managers, elder-law attorneys, move managers, financial planners, healthcare professionals, memory care specialists, and the nonprofit partners who serve them.
Will refreshments be served?
Always. Light refreshments are always thoughtfully provided by the host venue. In some gatherings, there are generous charcuterie spreads, fresh hors d'oeuvres, wine, and others. Take a look at the Gallery page for a sense of past spreads. Come hungry, come thirsty, come ready to talk.
Can I sponsor or host a future Mixer?
Yes — please. Hosting a Mixer is a beautiful way to introduce your community or service to peers in the senior-care space. Reach out via the registration form and we'll be in touch.
What's the dress code?
Business casual. Bring plenty of cards — and an open mind.
