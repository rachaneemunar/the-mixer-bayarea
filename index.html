About

Senior Pro Network

A monthly gathering where senior-care professionals on the Peninsula meet, connect, and grow together.

Our Story

Senior Pro Network was created by a small group of Peninsula professionals (Vivienne Kelvin, Jowett San Miguel, and Mark Martinho) who saw a need: a regular, welcoming space for the people who serve seniors to actually know each other.

Our inaugural Senior Pro Network event on April 1st, 2026 brought together business leaders, community partners, entrepreneurs, nonprofit professionals, and innovators, all united by a shared passion for supporting our senior community. The energy in the room was electric, and we knew we had something special.

What to Expect

Each Senior Pro Network event is hosted at a senior-living community or partner venue on the Peninsula. Expect a relaxed two-hour evening of warm conversation, light refreshments, and meaningful introductions.

No formal program. No sales pitches. Just real people who care about seniors, sharing what they do and learning from each other, the old-fashioned, trustworthy way of doing business.

Who Attends

A Community of Trusted Professionals

If you work in senior care, you belong here. Senior Pro Network is an open gathering for the professionals who serve older adults and their families: senior living and memory care, home care and healthcare, move management, financial planning, elder law, and everyone whose work touches the lives of seniors.

There's no membership to join and no invitation to wait for. Come as you are, bring your business cards, and spend an evening among peers who understand the work, trading referrals and resources, comparing notes, and building the relationships that help families find the right care at the right time.
